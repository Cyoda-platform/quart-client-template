hello, Build a application that compares today's gold rate with yesterday's gold rate and sends the report via email.

POST /job
Initiates the report creation process and stores the results as a "report."
Fetches the current (today’s) gold rate and the previous (yesterday’s) gold rate.
Compares the two rates and generates a report.
Sends an email with the gold rate comparison to the user.

GET /report by id
Retrieves the stored report by its ID.
Returns the previously generated gold rate comparison (today’s and yesterday’s rates).
